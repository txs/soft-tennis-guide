Here are some quality resources that can help you learn and improve your soft tennis skills:

## YouTube Channels

1. [@AndysTV Andy's Tennis Channel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nZYbjqNh5fM&list=PL1aVa65WLc50A-4G0wH2t4x70rSXS454S&pp=gAQBiAQB) - Provides rich soft tennis tutorial videos
2. Japan Soft Tennis Association Official Channel
3. Korean Soft Tennis Federation Channel

## Websites and Forums

1. [Reddit Soft Tennis Community](https://www.reddit.com/r/softtennis/) - International platform for soft tennis enthusiasts to exchange ideas
2. [PTT Soft Tennis Board](https://www.ptt.cc/bbs/SoftTennis/index.html) - Taiwan's largest soft tennis discussion board
3. [Japan Soft Tennis Association Official Website](https://www.jsta.or.jp/) - Learn about the development of soft tennis in Japan

## Applications

### iOS

1. [SwingVision: A.I. Tennis App](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/swingvision-tennis-pickleball/id989461317) - Uses AI technology to analyze and improve tennis skills, providing real-time feedback

2. [Nickelodeon Extreme Tennis](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/nickelodeon-extreme-tennis/id1536207521) - Nickelodeon's tennis game

3. [Tennis Open 2024: Clash Sport](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tennis-open-2024-clash-sport/id6476889326) - A newly launched tennis game app offering realistic match experiences and diverse game modes

4. [Tennis Champs Returns](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tennis-champs-returns/id1062318595) - A retro-style tennis game that combines role-playing and strategy elements

5. [Mini Tennis: Perfect Smash](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/mini-tennis-perfect-smash/id1615962417) - A simple and easy-to-play mini tennis game, suitable for casual entertainment and reaction training

### Android

1. [ATP Tour](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.atptour.playerzone&hl=en) - Track men's professional tennis tournaments and players

2. [ATP WTA Live](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.atptour.atptour&hl=en) - Track women's professional tennis tournaments and players

3. [Tennis Clash: Multiplayer Game](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tfgco.games.sports.free.tennis.clash) - Popular tennis game app

4. [Roland-Garros Official](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.netcosports.androlandgarros&hl=en) - Official app for the French Open tennis tournament

5. [Rules of Tennis](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.itftennis.rulesOfTennis&hl=en) - Official tennis rules app from the International Tennis Federation

6. [APEAK Tennis](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.apeak.tennis) - Tennis mental skills training

These applications cover various aspects from match tracking, skill improvement to finding tennis partners, which can comprehensively support your soft tennis learning and improvement. It's recommended to choose suitable applications based on personal needs and combine them with actual practice for the best results.
